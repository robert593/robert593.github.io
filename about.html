Ben Askren vs Robbie Lawlor Highlights

Controversy struck the anticipated Octagon debut of Ben Askren last night (Sat., Mar. 2, 2019) at UFC 235 live on pay-per-view (PPV) from inside T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas, Nevada, as “Funky” defeated former UFC welterweight champion Robbie Lawler via first-round submission (bulldog choke).

Askren had to endure a lot of early damage, including a “Ruthless” slam on his head, but he eventually took Lawler down. “Funky” was able to work to back control and quickly went for a rear-naked choke. Lawler turned over and nearly popped his head out, but Askren kept squeezing and caught the former champ in a bulldog choke. Lawler’s arm seemed to go limp and referee Herb Dean moved in to ask him if he’s okay. After a few seconds of no response Dean pulled Askren off, only to see Lawler quickly recover and protest the stoppage.
